Our Story

Prime Fitness was founded in 1982 as a family owned and operated business. And as the flagship brand for Prime Fitness Club Ltd, a UN based health and fitness services provider and a pioneer in the “Integrated Health Club Management Solutions’ business. Prime Fitness Club ltd focuses on staying fit is most important to meet the challenging requirements.

What We Do

We want to help people reach and maintain their goals, live a fit and healthy lifestyle!. We combine different types of fitness training sections to meet different fitness needs and levels.

VISION

Become the best and most prestigious quality in the Fitness industry, providing the best service and products to our customers.

MISSION

For Community: Being healthy and beautiful for a perfect life.
For Partners - Investors: Providing the reliable and sustainable values for Win-Win Cooperation.
For Employees: Creating an ideal working-environment where work and passion go hand in hand.

VALUE

Health is the first priority of life.
Credibility: Doing as we say - Prime Fitness always commits to building and maintaining the credibility with customers and partners.
