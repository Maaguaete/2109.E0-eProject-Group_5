FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

THE WORKOUTS LOOK HARD, WILL I BE ABLE TO KEEP UP?

Through the support and careful guidance of our coaches and heart rate tracking technology used, we’re able to adjust each individuals movements and intensity to ensure that all clients are working to their level. A thorough pre-screen is conducted and any injuries are accounted for with alternate movements. The workout is not competitive in terms of keeping up with a number of exercises, reps or speed. It’s progressive in nature and we support you in your training intensity.

AM I LOCKED INTO A LONG-TERM AGREEMENT?

At Prime Fitness we don’t believe in fixed term agreements, we think it’s important to exceed our clients expectations to keep them training with us, and not through forcing a locked in term period. If your situation changes and you need to stop the training plan, all we ask for is a fortnight notice. This will mean that there will be one final debit after giving notice, which of course during you will have normal access. Any questions on this don’t hesitate to ask.

IT SEEMS EXPENSIVE COMPARED TO A REGULAR GYM. WHAT’S THE DIFFERENCE?

Here a member pays their fee however attends on average less than once per week. They have little to no support without paying for a personal trainer. This quickly adds up to 100’s of $$ per week. We’ve found that our clients get amazing results because they have a personalised plan and clear training goals and support. It feels like having your own trainer however is much more affordable because it’s conducted in small groups. Our most popular plan the Unlimited Plan weekly fee is less than a dinner out for one.

DO I NEED TO BOOK IN FOR WORKOUTS?

Yes please. We have a maximum number of spots for each program and booking in ensures that you get the best workout experience possible. You can book in with the Prime Fitness App from the App store or Google Play. You can also book in online through our website. It’s quick, easy and gives you piece of mind. If you forget to book in and turn up for a session, we’ll of course look after you if spots are available however will ask you to wait till the commencement of session to join the group. If you have any problems booking let us know and we’ll fix it quickly for you.

HOW ABOUT NUTRITION? IS THERE ANY PROGRAM TO FOLLOW?

The adage you can’t out train a bad nutrition plan rings true. Effective weight loss requires not only regular exercise, it requires sustainable changes to your nutrition habits. Nutrition isn’t a one size fits all approach, so we offer personally tailored nutrition advice. No fad diets or quick fixes – our team will assist you in guidance in changing your eating habits in the long term. As an added service you can choose the successful GET LEAN program or more private consultations.

WHAT SHOULD I BRING TO EXERCISING AT PRIME FITNESS?

All you really need to bring along is your gym gear including shoes, a sweat towel and water bottle. We have filtered water fountain in the studio or you can purchase water, and healthy protein snacks if needed.
