What is Group Exercise

Before we jump into the advantages of group exercise classes, we’re going to quickly cover the answer to ‘what is group exercise?’ so that you know exactly what the exercise method entails! You may already have a strong idea of what it involves, but we’ll go over it just in case. Basically, all that the term alludes to is a form of exercise that is done as part of a group, be it large or small. It can mean any style of exercise, from H.I.I.T training to Pilates or Spinning, all the way to a football or netball team. Before you take a look at the 9 benefits of this exercise style, feel free to take a look at the benefits of group exercise study that we researched first! Hopefully, this will provide greater insight into the topic for you, and allow you to see how and why it has grown in popularity in recent years.
